    • bl-art

    • bl-art is a blockchain solution for the industry of Art and luxury goods. It offers a secure and transparent platform for tracking art items and products, insure quality and combat fraud.

    • Each asset (art or luxury good) on the blockchain is unique and represented by a unique token. Instead of dealing with cryptocurrency we are dealing with crypto-collectibles. Tokens are based on the ERC-721 standard which specifies a class of unique tokens.

    • Technology Stack:
      Ethereum + BigchainDB + IPFS + GatsbyJS (React Static PWA)
